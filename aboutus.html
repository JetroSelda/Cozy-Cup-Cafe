ABOUT US


ORIGIN AND HISTORY

Cozy Cup Café was established in 2018 by Maria and Juan Delacruz, a dynamic couple with a shared passion for coffee and community. Inspired by their travels across the Philippines and their love for local flavors, they envisioned a café that would blend the rich traditions of Filipino hospitality with a modern twist. After securing a charming location in the vibrant town of Makati, Maria and Juan set out to create a haven where locals and visitors alike could savor exceptional coffee and enjoy a warm, inviting atmosphere.

From its humble beginnings, Cozy Cup Café quickly gained popularity for its unique offerings and cozy ambiance. The café’s name reflects its mission to provide a comfortable and welcoming space where every visitor can feel right at home.




VALUES

Cozy Cup Café is built on a foundation of core values that guide every aspect of its operations:

Community: The café aims to be a heart of the community, a place where friends and families can gather and create lasting memories. Maria and Juan believe in fostering a strong sense of togetherness and supporting local initiatives, making the café a true reflection of Filipino warmth and spirit.

Sustainability: In line with their commitment to the environment, Cozy Cup Café prioritizes sustainable practices. They source coffee beans from local farmers, supporting fair trade and organic farming methods. The café also uses eco-friendly materials and implements waste reduction practices to minimize its ecological footprint.

Quality: At Cozy Cup Café, quality is paramount. The Delacruz family personally selects the finest beans from local and international roasters, ensuring that each cup of coffee meets the highest standards. The baristas are trained to deliver expertly crafted beverages, from rich espressos to smooth lattes, all made with a touch of Filipino artistry.




COMMITMENT TO QUIALITY

Cozy Cup Café’s dedication to quality extends beyond its coffee. The café features a menu of delicious Filipino-inspired pastries and snacks, made from locally sourced ingredients and prepared with care. The inviting interior, adorned with traditional Filipino decor and modern comforts, creates a perfect setting for relaxation and conversation.

In addition to serving exceptional coffee and food, Cozy Cup Café actively engages with the community by hosting events such as local art exhibitions, cultural performances, and workshops. These activities not only celebrate Filipino culture but also enhance the café’s role as a vibrant and supportive community space.

Cozy Cup Café is more than just a place to enjoy a great cup of coffee; it’s a beloved gathering spot that embodies the essence of Filipino hospitality, commitment to quality, and environmental stewardship.
